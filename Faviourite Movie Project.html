The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies

Baahubali

Baahubali is an epic tale of rivalry, revenge, and legacy set in the ancient kingdom of Mahishmati.

Pushpa

Pushpa is a gritty action drama about a determined laborer who rises through the ranks of the red sandalwood smuggling underworld.

KGF

KGF is a high-octane action drama about a fierce rebel who rises from poverty to power in the blood-soaked gold mines of Kolar.
